Services

NEED BASED SERVICE

At Bridgeway, we dont believe in the standard recovery treatment plan for substance abuse disorders and mental health concerns, issues that plague our communities. We know that the recovery process is 90% of the fight. That is why we develop a needs-based plan for each individual. We offer psychiatry, individual and group therapy, as well as medication management, case management services and TMS treatment. This comprehensive process has a proven track record that we are confident will help you succeed.

WELLNESS SUPPORT

Our team will support you in building a better you. No matter what your health needs are, having our Bridgeway team support you will keep you on the path to success. We work together to offer you the services you need.

FAMILY AND COMMUNITY HEALTH

When you choose Bridgeway, you join a group of professionals that genuinely care about your healing. We not only work with the client, but with other members of the family as well. We also work with members of the community to build a network of people working together for a healthier, happier you!
